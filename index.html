RPE to Weight Calculator

DISCLAIMER: The calculator above is intended for liters to approximate load based on given RPE and rep scheme and is NOT a concrete value that lifters must follow in their training; the given load can be adjusted higher or lower based on lifters' perceived physical condition/feedback and simply serves as a percentage approximation of what a lifter should load.

RPE percentage values are derived from a expanded version of Mike Tuscherer's RPE chart created by a user on the Exodus Strength forum https://www.exodus-strength.com/forum/viewtopic.php?t=2967
